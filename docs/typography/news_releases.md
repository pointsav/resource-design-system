# PointSav Digital Systems News Release Standards

This document defines the typographic rules for PointSav Digital Systems news releases, inherited from the COMM master standards.

## Core Rules
* **Alignment:** Left-aligned (ragged right) only. No justified text.
* **Capitalization:** Title Case for headers. No all-caps for PointSav Digital Systems or geographic locations.
* **Geography:** Use full spelling (e.g., Columbia Británica for Spanish syndication).

## Print Layout Standard
**For Immediate Release**

**[PointSav Headline in Title Case]**

**Vancouver, British Columbia – [Date] –** PointSav Digital Systems™ ("PointSav")...

## Web Layout Standard (HTML/CSS)
**PointSav Digital Systems™ — Comunicado de Prensa**

**[PointSav Headline in Title Case]**

**Para publicación inmediata | Vancouver, Columbia Británica | [Date]**
